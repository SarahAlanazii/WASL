How Wasl Works

Simple steps to transform your procurement process

1

Create Tender

Companies create detailed tenders with specifications and requirements. Our intuitive forms make it easy to include all necessary information.

2

Submit Bids

Suppliers review tenders and submit electronic bids with all required documents. The platform ensures all submissions are complete and on time.

3

Evaluate & Compare

System automatically evaluates and compares bids based on predefined criteria. Custom scoring algorithms help identify the best offers.

4

Award & Manage

Select the best bid, award contract, and manage the entire process through completion. Track performance and maintain relationships.
